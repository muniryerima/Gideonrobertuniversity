About us

Welcome to Eden University

Eden University, one of Zambia’s fastest-growing institutions, is dedicated to transforming communities and inspiring individuals through development, innovation, and collaborative pursuit of knowledge. Since our registration as a private university in 2016, we have rapidly expanded to six schools and a student body of approximately 7,000. Our mission is to transform communities, inspire individuals, and serve humanity through development, innovation, and collaborative knowledge pursuit..

Our Mission and Vision Statements

To contribute to transforming communities, inspiring and serving humanity through development, innovation, and collaborative pursuit of knowledge.

Eden University has successfully trained and graduated ten cohorts of students, many of whom have secured positions in local and international labor markets. We are committed to providing quality, cost-effective tertiary education, making it accessible and affordable for all. Our vision is to be an international Christian model of choice, dynamically shaping the future.

Our Core Values

At Eden University, our core values reflect our commitment to nurturing a holistic and inclusive community.

  • Christian Beliefs: Grounding our community in faith and moral integrity.
  • Accountability: Upholding transparency and responsibility in all our actions
  • Integrity: Maintaining honesty and strong moral principles.
  • Equity: Ensuring fairness and equal opportunities for all.
  • Academic and Professional Excellence: Striving for the highest standards in teaching, research, and service.
Academic Excellence

Academic Excellence

At Eden University, we offer a unique academic experience that includes lectures, seminars, tutorials, practical classes, and fieldwork. Our academic calendar is designed to provide a comprehensive education, empowering students with practical skills and knowledge. This model of learning ensures that our students are not restricted to the classroom but are prepared to tackle real-world challenges.

Message from the Vice Chancellor

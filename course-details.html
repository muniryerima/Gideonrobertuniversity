The Students are prepared for the future.

The primary objective of the school is to offer foundational training in core courses to students aspiring to careers in Medicine, Fire Engineering, Pharmacy, Nursing, Clinical Medical Sciences, and Environmental Health. Upon completing the foundational natural sciences curriculum, students can advance to specialized schools of their choice to further their professional education.

In the first year, students are introduced to entry-level natural sciences, designed to enhance their problem-solving abilities and equip them with a broad knowledge base and practical skills, preparing them for advanced training in their chosen fields..

Entry Requirements
  • High School Diploma or Equivalent: Applicants must have completed high school or hold an equivalent qualification, with a strong emphasis on science courses.
  • Standardized Test Scores: Submission of scores from standardized tests such as the MCAT (Medical College Admission Test) is required.
  • Prerequisite Courses: Completion of specific prerequisite courses, including Biology, Chemistry, Physics, and Mathematics, with a minimum grade requirement.
  • Letters of Recommendation: Applicants must provide letters of recommendation from academic or professional references who can attest to their suitability for a career in medicine.
  • Personal Statement: A personal statement or essay that outlines the applicant's motivation for pursuing a career in medicine, relevant experiences, and long-term career goals.
